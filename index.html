About Ricche

AI infrastructure designed to understand how financial markets work.

Financial markets produce enormous amounts of data every second — prices, volumes, order flows, economic indicators. Hidden within that data are patterns too complex for traditional analysis.

Ricche builds the tools to find those patterns. Our platform combines data pipelines, machine learning environments, market simulators, and validation systems into one research workflow.

The result: a structured, reproducible way to develop and test AI models that study market behaviour.

Founded in London

Ricche was founded by researchers with backgrounds in quantitative finance and machine learning, driven by the belief that AI infrastructure for market research should be rigorous, reproducible, and built from the ground up — not bolted onto legacy systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

What markets do you cover?

Our data infrastructure supports global equities, futures, options, FX, and fixed income. We ingest feeds from major exchanges and data providers, with coverage spanning developed and emerging markets.

What data sources do you use?

We work with institutional-grade market data — tick-level order books, end-of-day pricing, economic indicators, and alternative data sets. All data passes through quality checks before entering the research pipeline.

How do you handle data security?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Our infrastructure runs on isolated compute clusters with role-based access controls, audit logging, and strict data governance policies.

Who do you work with?

We partner with quantitative research teams, academic institutions, data scientists, and financial firms exploring AI-driven approaches to market analysis and research.

Can I access the platform for my own research?

Yes — we offer research partnerships for teams and individuals working at the intersection of AI and finance. Get in touch to discuss access and collaboration options.

What makes Ricche different from other platforms?

Ricche is built for rigour. Every experiment is versioned, every result is reproducible, and every model goes through structured validation before advancing. We're a research-first platform, not a trading tool.
